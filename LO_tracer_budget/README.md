
#### These two codes are used to calculate budget terms in heat/TN/DO in Salish Sea

- get_DO_bgc_air_sea_1.py
  calculate DO production from photosynthesis, DO consumption from water column remineralization+nitrification and sediment, air-sea O2 exchange, and DO*vol
  
- get_TNTempVol_Denitri_AirSeaHeat_1.py
  calculate TN*vol, temp*vol, air-sea heat exchange, and sediment TN loss (loss of TN from detritus sink out of the bottom layer - gain of TN from NH4 + loss of TN from denitrification)
